Traveling to Philadelphia

Getting to the city is easy: you can fly into Philadelphia International Airport (PHL), take a train to 30th Street Station, or drive via I-95 North or I-95 South.

If you plan to stay through the weekend, there’s plenty to do! The wedding hotel is located about 40 minutes from Center City Philadelphia, where you can find the Liberty Bell, Independence Hall, the Franklin Institute, South Street, and much more.
